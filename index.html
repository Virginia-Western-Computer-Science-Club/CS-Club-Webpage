Welcome to the VWCC Computer Science Club WebPage!

We are the Virginia Western Computer Sciences club. As a small group of passionate programmers and code enthusiasts, our group prides itself on our multifaceted approach to our various projects. With skill sets ranging from hobbyists and self taught enthusiasts to complete beginners looking to learn skills to take into the future. This varying level of skill and talent allows the CS Club to tackle projects ranging from website and software design, app development, fabrication and mechatronics and much, much more. In the CS Club, our only limit is our ambition. If you have the skills or interest in joining our team, reach out today!


Why should you join?

Have you ever been curious about how apps, games, or websites work? Maybe you love problem-solving, creative thinking, or just want to meet people who are passionate about technology. Whether you're a seasoned coder, a total beginner, or someone who just wants to explore, the CS Club is for you! We're not just about code, we're about community. We are always working on new projects and would love it for you to join! No tech background? No worries! Many of our members started with zero experience, and are now learning how to build their own projects. So, whether you're here to learn, build, or just hang out, there's a place for you in CS Club. Come for the knowledge, stay for the friendships. See you at our next meeting!
