Welcome to the VWCC Computer Science Club WebPage!

We are the Virginia Western Computer Sciences club. As a small group of passionate programmers and code enthusiasts, our group prides itself on our multifaceted approach to our various projects. With skill sets ranging from hobbyists and self taught enthusiasts to complete beginners looking to learn skills to take into the future. This varying level of skill and talent allows the CS Club to tackle projects ranging from website and software design, app development, fabrication and mechatronics and much, much more. In the CS Club, our only limit is our ambition. If you have the skills or interest in joining our team, reach out today!
